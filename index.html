Bangladeshi Classic Games Hub

Relive childhood favorites digitally: Chor Dakat, CricSpin, Qub, kaTakuTi!

Chor Dakat Babu Pulish

4-player guessing game with rotating roles. Pulish must find the culprit while others try to evade!

CricSpin

2-player cricket game with a 14-segment spinning wheel, coin toss, and customizable overs/wickets.

Qub – Word Battle

2-player word game on 10×10 grid. Form valid words, score points, and outsmart your opponent!

GoalDao

2-player strategic paper-football inspired passing game. Pass the ball, intercept opponents, and score goals!

LettR

A fast-paced, 2-player local word duel game. Test your vocabulary under pressure with random letters.

kaTakuTi

Two-player grid battle. Choose 3x3, 5x5, or 7x7 grids to compete for consecutive marks and win!
