How to Play

Objective

Score points by creating valid English words on the grid.

Rules

  • Timer: 15 seconds per turn. Miss it, and you lose 1 point!
  • Scoring: You get points for the longest valid word you form.
  • 2-Letter Words: Only standard dictionary words count (no abbreviations like 'st').
  • Unique Words: Repeating a word gives 0 points.
