# Bangladeshi Classic Games Hub

A digital hub for classic Bangladeshi childhood games: **CricSpin**, **Chor Dakat Babu Pulish**, **Qub**, and **kaTakuTi**.  
Relive nostalgic pen-and-paper and board games in modern web form with multiplayer support, interactive gameplay, and responsive UI.

##  Live
[https://swarup113.github.io/Bangladeshi-Classic-Games-Hub/](https://swarup113.github.io/Bangladeshi-Classic-Games-Hub/)

---

##  Games Included

| Game | Type | Description | Key Features | How to Play |
|------|------|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| **CricSpin** | 2-player cricket game | Spin-based cricket strategy game with coin toss, customizable overs/wickets, and a 14-segment spinning wheel | - Customizable team names & match settings (Overs: 1-50, Wickets: 1-10)<br>- Coin toss with bat/bowl choice<br>- 14-segment spinning wheel (Runs, Extras, Wickets)<br>- Track player stats: runs, balls, fours, sixes, overs<br>- Automatic Super Over for tie-breakers | 1. Open `CricSpin/index.html` in browser<br>2. Enter team names (optional)<br>3. Set overs & wickets<br>4. Click "Start Match"<br>5. Toss: Heads/Tails → choose Bat/Bowl<br>6. Spin the wheel and follow events |
| **Chor Dakat Babu Pulish** | 4-player guessing game | Pass-and-play game where roles rotate, and the Pulish (Police) must find the culprit while others evade capture | - Local multiplayer pass-and-play<br>- Random role assignment each round<br>- 30-second timer for guesses<br>- Score tracking across 10 rounds<br>- Dark theme UI & responsive design | 1. Open `Chor-Dakat-Babu-Pulish/index.html`<br>2. Enter player names (or use defaults)<br>3. Each player secretly views their role<br>4. Pulish has 30 seconds to guess the culprit<br>5. Points awarded based on results<br>6. Repeat 10 rounds; highest score wins |
| **Qub – Word Battle** | 2-player word game | Competitive 2-player word game on a 10×10 grid. Form valid words horizontally or vertically, score points, and outsmart your opponent | - 2-player grid-based word game<br>- 15-second timer per turn<br>- Dynamic scoring based on longest word<br>- Player-specific styling (Player 1: cyan, Player 2: coral)<br>- Repeated words give 0 points | 1. Open `Qub/index.html`<br>2. Enter player names<br>3. Take turns forming words on 10×10 grid<br>4. Longest valid words score higher points<br>5. Game continues for set turns; highest score wins |
| **GoalDao** | 2-player football game | A strategic paper-football inspired passing game. Pass the ball, intercept opponents, and score goals! | - 2-player grid-based football game<br>- Custom Match Time Duration <br>- teammates to pass or click the goal area to shoot.<br>- Team-specific styling (Team 1: Blue, Team 2: red)<br>- Score more goals than the opposition to win | 1. Open `GoalDao/index.html`<br>2. Enter Team names<br>3. Take turns by intercepting opposition's pass <br>4. The team with the most goals when the timer hits zero wins the match.|
| **kaTakuTi** | 2-player grid battle | Competitive two-player game. Choose grid size and compete to mark consecutive squares to win | - Two-player grid battle<br>- Choose grid size: 3×3, 5×5, or 7×7<br>- Track scores and rounds<br>- Responsive and compact design | 1. Open `kaTakuTi/index.html`<br>2. Enter player names<br>3. Choose grid size<br>4. Take turns marking squares<br>5. Get consecutive marks to win<br>6. Game ends after predetermined rounds or win condition |
